The Challenge

A child born today without access to technology in their formative years risks missing out on social and economic opportunities later in life. Over an entire childhood, the cost of adopting technology and keeping it up to date is too prohibitive to become ubiquitous - making the digital divide an inevitability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to prevent the digital divide by empowering parents and schools to adopt socially responsible technology, and ensure that technology reaches those who could not otherwise afford it - creating a fair start for all.

Our Story

Grown from the seed of One Laptop per Child, matured in Australian soil. One Education has been nurtured by Rangan and a collective of talented and passionate professionals who believe in the power of bringing technology to those most in need.

In eight years the organisation has transformed from a volunteer outfit to a full-time team that has delivered more than 50,000 XOs to children in Australia, New Zealand, Fiji, and the Philippines.

Now One Education strives to eliminate the digital divide and halve the long term cost of technology adoption by creating the Infinity - a modular computer for any child.
